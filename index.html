About Me

I am currently a Junior at the University of Washington's Information School, specializing in Human Computer Interaction. The Informatics program at the Information School offers me a plethora of skills such as: programming, designing, project management, networking, database management, and web development; similar to a Computer Science program but with a Human-Centered approach. Through my coursework I have experience with both the programming, designing, and research aspects in developing websites and mobile applications. I have developed programs in Java, C#, while also creating websites with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I also have experience with Relational Database Systems through Microsoft’s SQL Studio. Apart from my technical experience through coursework, I also have experience with working with teams and leadership. In my previous college, I worked as the Program Coordinator for El Centro Latino, a Student Program in Bellevue College (Bellevue, WA) which gave me the opportunity to direct this student program offering services to the college’s Latino students. There I managed the program’s finances, as well as managing projects such as: fundraisers, cultural events and educational trips while helping students in developing their academic and professional skills.

I have always developed a passion for tech; but also for music, sports and video games. Whether it is with my bass guitar or with my ukelele, in my free time I try to relax by playing some music and learning new songs. Coming from the island of Puerto Rico, baseball was my favorite sport growing up; but then I found a love for basketball, tennis, soccer and most recently, Football and Hockey. My favorite teams are the Seahawks, the Mariners, the Sounders and the Washington Huskies. As my passion for enjoying sports grew, my gaming interests shifted from Call of Duty, Halo and Assassins Creed to sports games like NBA 2K, EA Sports FIFA and Madden. I am currently trying to travel more around the world, as I haven't really been anywhere except from the US and Canada (and of course my casa, Puerto Rico). My goal for is to hopefully travel to Mexico this year.
